## About Me

I am an undergraduate student in the Honors Program in Computer Science at the [School of Data Science and Engineering](https://dase.ecnu.edu.cn/), [East China Normal University (ECNU)](https://www.ecnu.edu.cn/) (B.S. in Computer Science, 2023&nbsp;&ndash;&nbsp;2027 expected). Since June 2025, I have been a research intern at the [Decision Intelligence Lab](https://decisionintelligence.github.io/), advised by Prof. [Jilin Hu](https://hujilin1229.github.io/).

My research focuses on **efficient AI** — designing sub-quadratic attention mechanisms and discrete latent representations that scale to long contexts and high-dimensional generation, with the goal of making large generative models faster and lighter. I am also broadly interested in linear attention, inference acceleration, and generative modeling for time series.
